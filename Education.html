Educational Programme

As an organization T.D.A understands that Education is the bedrock for every development in TDA. We managed with the help of our partners to take one Turkana Student to K.F.C.B (KENYA FILMS CLASSIFICATION BOARD) school, Nairobi, for a full scholarship where he will study Films production course for 1 year. We have also managed to connect several students to industrial attachments to enable them to graduate from their respective Universities and colleges and taken 10 students to secondary school this year.
